Sarah

I’ve never written a letter like this.

It’s not a love letter.

It ain’t a hate letter either.

It’s a letter about hope

About the hope of us.

We might not work together.

But I hope we do.

It might not be easy or fast.

But I hope it will.

We probably will fight and maybe even cry.

And I hope we won’t.

But even if we do..

I hope you feel my soul coming to your soul

Mientras te apapacho.

And I think I was wrong from the beginning.

It is a love letter.

Because at the end.. Love is about hope.

And I hope about you. I hope about us.

I hope seeing sunsets and dancing in the ocean.

And maybe.. Just maybe..

A hopeful spirit can become a person’s reality.

Love,

Emmanuel
