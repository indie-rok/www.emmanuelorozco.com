This book changed my life.

I've been struggling with anxiety for over 10 years. I've tried a lot of stuff to fix it: meditation, therapy, yoga, exercise, travel, and more... But I could not fix it! 

And it was very paradoxical because the main learning of this book is: In order to fix your anxiety, you have to stop trying to fix your anxiety.

After reading this, I felt a big relief in my chest. I don't have to fix anything; anxiety is a feeling, like any other feeling. Accept it and learn to live with it. After some time, the anxiety will go away.

I am still not cured, hell, I am even feeling a bit anxious while writing this, but the difference is that it no longer controls most of my life like it used to.

100% recommended to anyone struggling with anxiety everyday.
